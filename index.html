About us

ITS Service is a part of Southeast Queensland IT Shop high-tech retail network. With 5 stores along the coast we offer last software, computers, and all range of handheld devices and accesorises for our customers.

Our specialisation is service and repair work to the local community for both home users and small business on various devices. This includes but are not limited to laptops, desktop computers, tablets and all Apple products line: iPods, iPads, iPhones and Macbooks. Repairs are also carried out on mobile phones such as Samsung, HTC, Nokia and Blackberry.

Our qualified technicians repair devices with the highest quality of parts. We are proud to qualify our repair service as fast and reliable with the most competitive prices on the coast.
